Who am I?

Hi, my name is Fidel Boamah. I am currently a sophomore at Drexel University, pursuing a Bachelor's of Science degree in Interactive Digital Media.

I modelled this website after Sean Haplin's portfolio site. I wanted to focus on trendy minimalist designs and user experience.

The main tools I used to build this site are VScode, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

In case you're wondering what I look like, that's me up there.


Aside my pursuit for higher education. I am an artist at heart. I enjoy drawing and designing things that people love and crave to interact with.
